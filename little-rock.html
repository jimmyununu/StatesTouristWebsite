About Little Rock

Little Rock, the capital of Arkansas, is located in the central part of the state. It has a rich history and serves as a major hub for business, government, and culture.

Capital City Facts

  • Population: 202,591 (2023 estimate)
  • Incorporation Year: 1831
  • Region: Central Arkansas
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $47,597 (compared to state average of $43,000)

Did You Know?

Every president gets their own library and the city of Little Rock is home to the William J. Clintion Presidential Libbrary
